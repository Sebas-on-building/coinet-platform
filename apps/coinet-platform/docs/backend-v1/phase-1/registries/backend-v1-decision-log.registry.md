## Scope of This Log

Records here are limited to **major decisions** that:

- accept a Plan 1.x document,
- approve a non-trivial SCR,
- amend a Plan 1.x document outside an SCR (rare, requires Plan 1.1 §13),
- record an Authoritative Decision Record (ADR) that does not fit into BTAR/AFE/VSE/FRP/BSCP/UDF.

Routine BTARs, exception approvals, and individual task admissions are **not** logged here. They live in their respective registries (`backend-v1-record-index.registry.md`, `backend-v1-active-task.registry.md`, `backend-v1-exception.registry.md`).

## ADR Records

Detailed Authoritative Decision Records live under:

```text
phase-1/records/decisions/ADR-NNN-short-slug.md
```

This registry indexes them chronologically; the record-index registry indexes them by ID.

## Append-Only

This log is append-only. Past entries are never deleted. If a decision is reversed, the reversal is a new entry referencing the prior decision.
