# Coinet AI Voice & Tone Guide

## Brand Voice

### Confident & Knowledgeable
- Use clear, definitive language for market insights
- Present data objectively without emotional language
- Acknowledge uncertainty when appropriate: "Based on current data..." 

### Professional but Approachable
- Avoid crypto jargon unless necessary (define when used)
- Use active voice: "Monitor BTC price" not "BTC price should be monitored"
- Be direct: "Create alert" not "You might want to consider creating an alert"

### Non-Hype, Evidence-Based
- ❌ "Bitcoin to the moon!" 
- ✅ "BTC shows bullish momentum with 15% weekly gain"
- Focus on facts and actionable insights
- Avoid superlatives like "amazing," "incredible," "revolutionary"

## Tone Guidelines

### Error Messages
- **Empathetic**: "We couldn't connect to the market data. Let's try again."
- **Solution-focused**: Always provide next steps or alternatives
- **Honest**: Don't hide problems behind vague messages

### Success States  
- **Encouraging**: "Alert created successfully. We'll notify you when BTC hits $50,000"
- **Specific**: Include relevant details and confirmation
- **Forward-looking**: Suggest what the user might do next

### Loading States
- **Informative**: "Fetching latest market data..." 
- **Time-aware**: "This usually takes 5-10 seconds"
- **Engaging**: Teach something useful while waiting

## Content Patterns

### Empty States
Format: **Friendly Problem + Clear Action + Quick Benefit**
- "No alerts yet. Create your first alert to stay updated on market moves."
- "No agents configured. Build a custom agent to automate your analysis workflow."

### Button Text
- Use verbs: "Create Alert" not "Alert Creation"
- Be specific: "Save Strategy" not just "Save"
- Show impact: "Enable Notifications" not "Continue"

### Error Recovery
- **Acknowledge**: "Connection lost"
- **Explain**: "Market data requires a stable connection"  
- **Action**: "Retry Connection" button
- **Alternative**: "View Cached Data" option

## Example Applications

### AI Chat Interface
- **Greeting**: "Ask me anything about crypto markets"
- **Processing**: "Analyzing current market conditions..."
- **Results**: "Here's what the data shows:" (then specific insights)

### Alert System
- **Creation**: "Set up smart monitoring for your portfolio"
- **Trigger**: "🚨 BTC Alert: Price dropped 5% to $45,230"
- **Management**: "3 active alerts monitoring your positions"

### Agent Builder
- **Onboarding**: "Custom agents help automate your trading analysis"
- **Building**: "Choose your agent's focus area and data sources"
- **Running**: "Agent 'DeFi Scanner' found 2 new opportunities"
